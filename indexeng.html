About me

My name is Jesus Rioja Bravo. I did an Informatic Engineering degree in Cordoba (Spain) with double mention at Computing & Hardware. After finishing it, I started to work at the University (UCO) as Support Investigator meanwhile I was finishing a post-degree about videogames programming.

During the last few years (about 2018) I have started to develop some videogames on my own, and I have also participated in many Jams. Most of my projects have been developed on Unity, but actually, thanks to the post-degree, I have also learnt about developing in Unreal (blueprints and C++) and I have made some projects on this engine too. I consider myself to have a pretty decent level on both engines.

I am a really passionate person about videogames, willing to learn as much as I can and always try my best. I am a communicative person that never hesitates to ask if I have to. Most of the projects I made have been developed in groups, so I am used to work with other people.

Experienced on C, C# and C++. Good knowledge on engines such as Unity y Unreal Engine 5 . I am also skillfull on other languages, like Python or Java. Experienced on GIT too.

Shadow Clone

Shadow Clone Puzzle videogame in which you have to make use of 2 characters, each with unique mechanics. With the aim of combining the characteristics of both of them to overcome the different levels of the game. Videogame developed as the Final Proyect of a videogame programming master. Made completely on C++. All the actors and character mechanics have been developed by me, in addition to the widgets desing and visual components.

Heart of the Forest

Heart of the Forest An isometric tower defense, where you embody the spirit guardian of the forest, who must protect the pond from the incoming attacks of the fungal kingdom. This game was created during the TecnoCampus Mataró GGJ23 where we received the award of best music and sound. Enemies behaviour and waves management system developed by me.

Raíces

Raíces Protege el legado de tu familia, es lo que tu abuelo hubiera querido. Raíces es un juego de gestión de recursos y toma de decisiones, en el que tendrás que cuidar un pequeño brote hasta llegar a convertirse en un gran árbol centenario. Te invitamos a jugarlo varias veces para descubrir todos los eventos posibles. Este proyecto ha sido fruto de la participación en la Indie Spain Jam 2022, donde ha sido premiado en la categoría "mejor uso del tema". On this project, I programmed the functionality of the different events that trigger during the game, in addition toe the manager of those events.

Pequeñas Sospechas

Pequeñas Sospechas "Pequeñas Sospechas" is a mini game developed by Pego Games for the Málaga Game Jam Weekend 14. Agent Rodríguez receives a call, her co-worker needs to be covered by doing lineups at the police station. You must read the reports and consult the testimonies of the suspects to determine who was the culprit in each case. But beware, nothing is what it seems. I was the lead programmer of this project. I developed all the systems and the game loop.
